FACTS

#1 How You Doin’ :

Since the beginning of the show, we have seen so many ladies around Joey that we cannot count them on our fingers. His How You Doin is still one of the best pick-up lines ever made. No doubt Joey was a Ladies man.

#2 One Of The Eight Sibling:

Everybody knows Joey Tribbiani had a big family, but seven sisters dude? That’s pretty crazy. It might not have been difficult for him but it definitely got Chandler into trouble. LOL!

#3 Matt LeBlanc Hated Joey & Rachel’s Affair :

There might be a lot of people who ship Joey and Rachel’s relationship. But there is also a whole different group of people who just hated their relationship. To the latter ones, congratulations, Matt LeBlanc is also in the same group

#4 Joey’s Character Was The Last To Be Finalized:

Among all the six leads of FRIENDS, Joey’s character was the last one to be finalized. Matt LeBlanc had only $11 when he was selected and from his first paycheck, the first thing that he bought was dinner. The struggle was common in both Joey’s life and Matt LeBlanc’s life.
