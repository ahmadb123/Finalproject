Favorite Book Little Women

Throughout Friends, Rachel is never seen as much of a reader, but she does have a favorite book. In season 3, episode 13, we discover that not only is Joey's favorite book The Shining by Stephen King, but Rachel's favorite read is Little Women by Louisa May Alcott. She admits that Little Women is the only book she's ever read more than one time attempt to drive Rachel back to her..
