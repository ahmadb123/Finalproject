Ross Geller

Famous We Were On A Break

Why He Slept With Xerox Girl

We’d heard rumors about the Xerox girl from Chandler and Joey in the early stages of Friends, but none of us could’ve ever imagined that it would be Ross who slept with her..

Not Accepting Full Responsibility

Not long after Ross and Rachel broke up for the first time, it appeared as if they’d been able to settle their differences. However, after finally reading her incredibly long letter and realizing that she wanted him to take full responsibility for their break-up, Ross’ ego just wouldn’t let her get away with that.

Divorce Teasing

Ross has had to go through the process of getting a divorce with three different women: Carol, Emily, and Rachel. Despite the fact that two of those two instances were very serious incidents that seemed to seriously hamper his personal life for quite some time, the gang always found it funny to mock him relentlessly about it..
